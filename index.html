McLaren Synthetic Reeds

McLaren Synthetic Reeds are designed to provide reliable performance, consistent tone and ease of use for pipers playing in a wide range of conditions.

Highland Chanter Reeds

Synthetic reeds have been available in recent times for a variety of woodwind instruments. Until the introduction of the McLaren Synthetic Reed, development to suit Highland Bagpipe chanters and achieve correct tone and response had proven difficult.

The advantages of McLaren Synthetic Reeds over traditional cane reeds lie in their reliability. They perform consistently in conditions where cane reeds can be unpredictable, particularly in severe weather such as humidity, heat and cold.

Because McLaren Synthetic Reeds are almost instantly in tune when correctly set up, very little “tune-up” time is required. This makes them particularly useful in situations where the reed needs to be playable straight out of the box.

McLaren Synthetic Reeds are not intended to replace a good cane reed in the competition scene. However, because their performance and sound are almost indistinguishable from cane, many pipers choose them to enjoy consistent playing without the ongoing manipulation that cane reeds can require.

Testimonials from satisfied pipers confirm that the McLaren Synthetic Reed is a viable alternative that allows more pleasurable and dependable playing.

These reeds can be played at much lower pressure while retaining good volume. This allows pipers to play an easy set of pipes with satisfactory musical results.

For beginners and older pipers in particular, the McLaren Synthetic Reed offers a usable alternative to sometimes troublesome cane reeds.

Border Chanter Reeds

Developed from the Highland chanter reed, McLaren Synthetic Border Chanter Reeds provide a very low-pressure option that performs well on Border Pipes in the key of A or G.

These reeds have a vibrancy and tone closer to Highland Pipes, while retaining the defining characteristics of a Border chanter.

They can be played using either bellows or mouth-blown systems, delivering consistent and reliable results.

For outdoor or stage performance, these reeds offer a sound and tonality that blends well with other instruments.

Overall performance, particularly on the bottom hand, allows Highland gracing without difficulty.

In summary, the McLaren Synthetic Reed provides a dependable and musically satisfying alternative to cane Border chanter reeds.
